## 📖 Overview

<a href="#">Suno</a> produces complete original songs — vocals, lyrics, melody, instrumentation, and production — from text descriptions in seconds. The gap between having a musical idea and hearing a finished song has historically required either years of musical training or collaboration with professional musicians and producers. Suno eliminates that gap entirely.

The <a href="#">text-to-music generation engine</a> interprets genre descriptions, mood indicators, lyrical themes, instrumentation preferences, and stylistic references and produces complete tracks that reflect the described intent. Describe a melancholic indie folk song about a late autumn drive, a high-energy EDM track for a gym playlist, or a smooth jazz instrumental for background listening, and Suno generates a finished song with appropriate production quality for each. <a href="#">Vocal generation</a> creates realistic singing voices that match the genre and emotional register of the track, with natural phrasing, breath timing, and expressive dynamics rather than robotic synthesis.

<p align="center">
  <img src="https://cdn-o.suno.com/studio-landing-page-any-audio.webp" alt="Suno screenshot"/>

<a href="#">Custom lyric input</a> generates songs around specific words, lines, or full lyric sets that you provide, building melody, arrangement, and production around your written content. Write your own lyrics and let Suno handle everything else required to make them a song. <a href="#">Genre and style mixing</a> combines multiple genre references in a single prompt to produce hybrid styles — country with trap production elements, classical melodies with electronic arrangement, folk vocals with cinematic orchestration — that would be difficult to produce through conventional music production.

The <a href="#">song extension capability</a> continues a generated track beyond its initial length, adding new sections that follow the established musical direction while developing the song's structure. Build full-length songs by extending promising initial generations. <a href="#">Audio download</a> exports generated tracks as audio files for use in video projects, presentations, content creation, personal playlists, and any other application where original music without licensing complications is needed.

## 📌 FAQ

<details>
<summary>What music genres can Suno generate?</summary>

Suno covers a very wide genre range including pop, rock, hip-hop, EDM, jazz, classical, folk, country, metal, R&B, and many more. Genre mixing and hybrid styles are also supported through combined genre references in prompts.

</details>

<details>
<summary>Can I use Suno-generated music commercially?</summary>

Commercial use rights depend on the subscription plan. Paid plans typically include commercial licensing for generated tracks. Review the current terms of service for the specific rights applicable to your plan before using generated music in commercial contexts.

</details>

<details>
<summary>How long are generated songs?</summary>

Initial generations typically produce tracks ranging from one to two minutes. The song extension feature continues tracks beyond the initial generation. Full-length songs can be built through sequential extension of promising initial outputs.

</details>

<details>
<summary>Can Suno generate music in a specific artist's style?</summary>

Suno can generate music inspired by artist styles and genres without reproducing copyrighted recordings. Describe the stylistic characteristics you want rather than naming specific tracks, which produces original compositions in that direction.

</details>
